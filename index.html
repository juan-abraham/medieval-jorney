MEDIEVAL JOURNEY

Medieval Journey aims to provide our clients with a spiritual journey back in the years where alcoholic beverages as we know them today were developed. We have developed very good jobs and made our own recipes, which will transport you to Ancient Greece, through our different Meads, or to Medieval England through our beers. We are two friends who, tired of drinking bad beer, decided to make their own, for this reason we decided to take different courses and train as Master Brewers.
